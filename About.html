About

Why does this website exist? That's a great question. One day, someone decided the internet needed one more website. Instead of making a social network, productivity app, or cryptocurrency project, they chose beetles. This site celebrates the tiny armored creatures that somehow conquered almost every corner of the planet. Here you'll find beetle facts (don't mind there not being a general "Beetle Lore" page, I've still yet to make that), species information, and occasional reminders that beetles are far more successful than humans from an evolutionary perspective. Enjoy your stay, and remember: every beetle is somebody's favorite beetle (probably except for flour beetles, they're lame😒).
